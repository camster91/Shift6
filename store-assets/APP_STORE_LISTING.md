## Full Description

**Transform your fitness in 6 weeks with science-based bodyweight training.**

Shift6 is your personal bodyweight fitness coach. Master 9 foundational exercises through structured, progressive workouts designed to build real strength - no gym required.

**KEY FEATURES**

• **Smart Progression System** - Automatic difficulty scaling based on your performance
• **9 Essential Exercises** - Push-ups, Squats, Pull-ups, Dips, V-Ups, Glute Bridges, Planks, Lunges, and Supermans
• **Personalized Programs** - Choose from templates or build your own workout plan
• **Home & Gym Modes** - Flexible training that adapts to your environment
• **Express Workouts** - Quick 10-15 minute sessions for busy schedules
• **Achievement System** - Earn badges and track personal records
• **100% Offline** - All data stays on your device, no account required
• **Dark Mode** - Beautiful interface designed for focus

**HOW IT WORKS**

1. Complete a quick assessment to find your starting point
2. Follow daily workouts with automatic rest timers
3. Track your progress with detailed stats and calendar view
4. Celebrate achievements as you level up each exercise

**EXERCISE LIBRARY**

Each exercise includes:
- Clear form instructions
- Video demonstrations
- Difficulty progressions
- Personal record tracking

**PRIVACY FIRST**

Your workout data never leaves your device. No accounts, no tracking, no ads.

**PERFECT FOR**

- Beginners starting their fitness journey
- Experienced athletes maintaining strength while traveling
- Anyone wanting effective home workouts
- People who prefer workout tracking without subscriptions

Download Shift6 and start your transformation today!
